IoT StreamConf Privacy Policy

The only personal information we collect on this site is your name, address, email, phone and related contact information. In addition, we collect and store non-personally identifiable information including the IP address from where you accessed the site, the type of browser and the date and time you access our site.

We may disclose your personal information for the following purposes:

  1. To IoT StreamConf contractors to provide services to you;
  2. To protect the site and our rights; to protect ourselves against liability or prevent fraudulent activity; or where it is necessary to permit us to pursue available remedies or limit any damages that we may sustain;
  3. If required to comply with a subpoena or warrant issued or an order made by a court, person or body with jurisdiction to compel the production of information, or to comply with the rules or court relating to the production of records; and
  4. If we believe in good faith that a law, regulation, rule or guideline requires it.

Changes to privacy policy

We may change or supplement this Privacy Policy from time to time and we will post revised versions of this Policy on the site. Privacy Policy changes will apply to the information collected from the date we post the revised Privacy Policy to the site, as well as to existing information held by us.
